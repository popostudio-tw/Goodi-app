隱私權政策

最後更新日期：2025年12月30日

本隱私權政策說明了 Goodi（“我們”）如何收集、使用和揭露您的資訊。本隱私權政策適用於所有我們的服務和應用程式（統稱為“服務”）。

1. 我們收集的資訊

我們可能收集的個人資訊包括：

  • 您提供的資訊： 當您註冊帳戶、建立或修改個人資料、設定偏好、透過我們的服務進行購買、或以其他方式與我們溝通時，您可能會提供個人資訊，例如您的姓名、電子郵件地址、電話號碼和支付資訊。
  • 使用服務自動收集的資訊： 當您使用我們的服務時，我們會自動記錄資訊，包括您使用的服務類型、設定、以及您的 IP 位址、瀏覽器類型、作業系統、推薦/退出頁面、日期/時間戳。

2. 我們如何使用您的資訊

我們使用您的資訊的目的包括：

  • 提供、操作和維護我們的服務。
  • 改進、個人化和擴展我們的服務。
  • 了解和分析您如何使用我們的服務。
  • 開發新產品、服務、功能和能力。
  • 出於合規目的，包括執行我們的服務條款，或法律要求或法院命令可能要求的其他法律權利。

3. 我們如何分享您的資訊

我們不會與第三方分享您的個人資訊，除非在以下情況之一：

  • 經您同意。
  • 用於法律原因：如果我們真誠地相信存取、使用、保存或揭露資訊是合理必要的，以滿足任何適用法律、法規、法律程序或可執行的政府要求。

4. 您的權利

根據您所在的位置，您可能擁有某些關於您個人資訊的權利，包括存取、更正或刪除您的個人資訊的權利。

5. 第三方服務提供商

為了提供我們的服務，我們使用以下第三方服務：

  • Firebase（由 Google 提供）：用於用戶認證、數據存儲（Firestore）、雲端函數和託管。您的數據將根據 Google 隱私權政策進行處理。
  • Gemini API（由 Google 提供）：用於 AI 互動功能，包括每日總結、智慧回應和學習建議。

重要承諾：我們不會將兒童的個人數據出售給第三方，也不會用於建立用戶行為檔案或投放廣告。

6. AI 數據處理說明

Goodi 使用 AI 技術來提供個性化的學習體驗和情緒支持。關於兒童輸入的內容（如樹洞心事、日記、任務描述），我們做出以下承諾：

  • 僅用於服務功能：兒童輸入的文字或語音內容僅用於生成 AI 互動回應、每日總結和家長報告，不會被用於其他目的。
  • 不用於 AI 訓練：您的孩子的數據不會被用於訓練通用的 AI 模型，也不會與其他用戶的數據混合使用。
  • 臨時處理：AI 處理請求是即時的，處理後的原始輸入不會永久存儲在 AI 服務提供商的服務器上。
  • 家長控制：家長可以隨時查看孩子的所有數據，並要求刪除。

7. 兒童隱私保護

我們的服務主要設計給家長和監護人管理，我們鼓勵家長積極參與和監督孩子的線上活動。我們承諾：

  • 家長同意：我們不會故意在未經父母或監護人同意的情況下收集 13 歲以下兒童的個人可識別資訊。
  • COPPA 合規：我們遵守美國《兒童網上隱私保護法》(COPPA) 和歐盟 GDPR-K（兒童數據保護）規範。
  • 數據最小化：我們僅收集提供服務所必需的最少數據，不收集廣告識別碼 (IDFA) 或用於行為追蹤的數據。
  • 即時刪除：如果我們發現在未經父母同意的情況下收集了兒童的個人資料，我們將立即採取措施從我們的伺服器上刪除該資訊。

8. 帳號與數據刪除

家長或監護人有權隨時要求刪除孩子的帳號和所有相關數據：

  • App 內刪除：在 App 的「家長模式」中，您可以找到「刪除帳號」選項，點擊後將永久刪除所有數據。
  • 電郵申請：您也可以發送電子郵件至 popo.studio@msa.hinet.net，我們將在 30 天內完成刪除。
  • 刪除範圍：刪除將包括所有個人資料、任務記錄、日記內容、樹洞心事、成績數據、AI 互動歷史和備份數據。
  • 不可恢復：數據刪除後無法恢復，請在操作前確認。

9. 無廣告與行為追蹤

為保護兒童隱私，我們做出以下承諾：

  • 無第三方廣告：本 App 不包含任何第三方行為廣告或廣告網路。
  • 無追蹤技術：我們不使用 Cookie、像素標籤或其他追蹤技術來收集用戶行為數據。
  • 無 IDFA：我們不收集廣告識別碼（IDFA）或任何用於跨應用追蹤的識別符。
  • 功能性數據：我們自動收集的資訊（如 IP 位址、設備類型）僅用於服務的正常運作和安全防護，不用於廣告投放或用戶畫像。

10. 聯繫我們

如果您對本隱私權政策有任何疑問，請透過電子郵件聯繫我們：popo.studio@msa.hinet.net

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-12-30

This Privacy Policy describes how Goodi ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your information. This Privacy Policy applies to all of our services and applications (collectively, the "Services").

1. Information We Collect

Personal information we may collect includes:

  • Information You Provide: You may provide personal information when you sign up for an account, create or modify your profile, set preferences, make purchases through, or otherwise communicate with us through our Services, such as your name, email address, phone number, and payment information.
  • Information Collected Automatically Through Use of the Services: We automatically record information when you use our Services, including the types of Services you use, your settings, and your IP address, browser type, operating system, referring/exit pages, and date/time stamps.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for purposes including:

  • To provide, operate, and maintain our Services.
  • To improve, personalize, and expand our Services.
  • To understand and analyze how you use our Services.
  • To develop new products, services, features, and functionality.
  • For compliance purposes, including enforcing our Terms of Service, or other legal rights as may be required by applicable laws and regulations or requested by any judicial process or governmental agency.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties except in one of the following circumstances:

  • With your consent.
  • For legal reasons: If we have a good-faith belief that access, use, preservation or disclosure of the information is reasonably necessary to meet any applicable law, regulation, legal process or enforceable governmental request.

4. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, including the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information.

5. Third-Party Service Providers

To provide our Services, we use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (provided by Google): Used for user authentication, data storage (Firestore), cloud functions, and hosting. Your data will be processed according to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Gemini API (provided by Google): Used for AI interaction features, including daily summaries, smart responses, and learning suggestions.

Important Commitment: We will never sell children's personal data to third parties, nor use it to build user behavior profiles or serve advertisements.

6. AI Data Processing Disclosure

Goodi uses AI technology to provide personalized learning experiences and emotional support. Regarding content entered by children (such as tree hole confessions, journals, task descriptions), we make the following commitments:

  • Service Functionality Only: Text or voice content entered by your child is used solely to generate AI interaction responses, daily summaries, and parent reports, and for no other purposes.
  • Not Used for AI Training: Your child's data is not used to train general-purpose AI models, nor is it mixed with other users' data.
  • Temporary Processing: AI processing requests are real-time, and the original input is not permanently stored on the AI service provider's servers after processing.
  • Parental Control: Parents can view all of their child's data at any time and request deletion.

7. Children's Privacy Protection

Our Services are designed to be managed by parents and guardians, and we encourage parental involvement and supervision of children's online activities. We commit to:

  • Parental Consent: We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13 without parental or guardian consent.
  • COPPA Compliance: We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and the EU GDPR-K (children's data protection) regulations.
  • Data Minimization: We only collect the minimum data necessary to provide our services and do not collect advertising identifiers (IDFA) or data used for behavioral tracking.
  • Immediate Deletion: If we discover that we have collected a child's personal data without parental consent, we will immediately take steps to remove that information from our servers.

8. Account and Data Deletion

Parents or guardians have the right to request deletion of their child's account and all related data at any time:

  • In-App Deletion: In the App's "Parent Mode," you can find the "Delete Account" option, which will permanently delete all data upon confirmation.
  • Email Request: You may also email popo.studio@msa.hinet.net, and we will complete the deletion within 30 days.
  • Deletion Scope: Deletion will include all personal information, task records, journal entries, tree hole confessions, grade data, AI interaction history, and backup data.
  • Irreversible: Data cannot be recovered after deletion, please confirm before proceeding.

9. No Advertising or Behavioral Tracking

To protect children's privacy, we make the following commitments:

  • No Third-Party Advertising: This App does not contain any third-party behavioral advertising or ad networks.
  • No Tracking Technologies: We do not use cookies, pixel tags, or other tracking technologies to collect user behavior data.
  • No IDFA: We do not collect advertising identifiers (IDFA) or any identifiers used for cross-app tracking.
  • Functional Data: Information we collect automatically (such as IP address, device type) is used only for the normal operation and security of our services, not for advertising or user profiling.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us by email at: popo.studio@msa.hinet.net
